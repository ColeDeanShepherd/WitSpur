Calculating your GPA by hand is simple but tedious:

  1. For each class, look up the number of grade points you've earned in the table below, and multiply by the number of credit hours.
  2. Add those numbers up.
  3. Divide the sum of those numbers by the sum of the credit hours for each of your classes.
