About WitSpur's Pomodoro Timer

Boost your productivity with WitSpur's free Pomodoro Timer.

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management technique invented by Francesco Cirillo. The technique consists of 25-minute blocks of focused, distraction-free work called pomodoros followed by 5-minute breaks, and a longer 20-30 minute break after every four pomodoros.

This simple technique keeps you energized and focused, assists with time-tracking and time-estimation, and prevents unhealthy periods of prolonged sitting.
