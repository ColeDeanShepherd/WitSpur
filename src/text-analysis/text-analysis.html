About WitSpur's Character Counter, Word Counter, & Line Counter

This is a free web tool to compute counts and frequencies of letters and other characters, words, and lines. Our tool is free of intrusive ads, works well with mobile-devices, and focuses on simplicity and performance.

Use this tool for:

  • ensuring character limits aren't exceeded for Twitter (140 characters), SMS (160 characters), etc.
  • checking if essays or short stories meet word count requirements for school assignments or website/journal submissions
  • search engine optimization with keyword counts and keyword density analysis
  • improve your writing by identifying overused or weak words
  • breaking Caesar ciphers and other simple substitution ciphers

and more.
